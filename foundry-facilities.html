Foundry Facilities

We provide a unique ability to respond rapidly to customer requirements and manufacture high quality components in wide range of grades in grey iron. We also have the ability to manufacture the most intricately shaped castings ranging from the size of a 1 kg piece up to 3000 Kg in weight as one offs or in large production batches. We can provide complete engineering, design and development service or alternatively, can simply use your existing patterns. When customers choose to work with us, they do so with an assurance of knowing they have selected a manufacturing partner that will support them long into the future. Our success has been built upon a unique ability to deliver an unmatched service & assured quality by working with customers throughout every project.
