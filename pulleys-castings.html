Cast Iron Pulleys Castings

We manufacture all types of Pulleys like V-Belt, Taperlock etc. Our Pulleys are available both in rough casting state as well as finished castings. We do pulleys as per the drawings or patterns supplied to us by our customers. Pulleys are available in cast iron, graded alloy casting & cast steel. Many suppliers in Eastern India are sourcing their pulleys from us.

Pulleys are of the following types:-

  • Flat Pulley
  • Split Crown Face Pulley
  • Split Pulley
  • V Pulley
  • Motor Crown Face Pulley

Our Castings are Characterized as:-

  • Dimensional Accuracy.
  • Precision Engineering and Durability.
  • Better Surface Finish.
  • Affordable Prices
  • Superior -Premium Raw Material.
