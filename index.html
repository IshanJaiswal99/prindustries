With a Manufacturing experience of around 30+ years, P. R. Industries has become a pioneer in the production and supplying of premium grade grey castings.

Our Customer-Focussed approach, highly responsive service, and world-class quality products justifies our sustenance in this competitive market. We understand the need for organizations to work with trusted suppliers and therefore, we provide a reliable, cost-effective products aimed at achieveing 100% customer satisfaction.

We have been manufacturing castings since 1980's and call upon unmatched proficiency to engineer bespoke solutions for customers representing most industry sectors. We understand that "Commitment to excellence begins at source".

Our excellence is marked by a unique ability to deliver an outstanding service & assured quality by working with customers throughout each project. We can also provide the complete engineering, design and development service or alternatively, simply use your existing patterns. When customers choose to work with us therefore, they do so with the assurance partner who will support them long into the future.

We have the expertise to engineer the complete competitive, value for money solution. In addition to selecting the most cost-effective method of manufacture, we call upon extensive on site logistical capabilities to produce components in large volumes and store them on behalf of customers.

We are able to take advantage of resources, research, design and development capabilities that are simply beyond most other foundries. Our fully integrated design and prototyping facilities include CAD/CAM & SolidWorks modeling that is directly linked to CNC pattern making equipment both in wood and aluminum. . We also take the advantage of 3D Scanning as well as 3D printing in our projects.

  • High ratio of first time right development.
  • Dedicated CAD CAM 3D scanning & designing team.
  • Reduced lead time.
