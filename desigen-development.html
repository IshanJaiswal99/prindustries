Design & Development

We are able to take advantage of resources, research, design and development capabilities that are simply beyond most other foundries. Our fully integrated design and prototyping facilities include CAD/CAM & SolidWorks modeling that is directly linked to CNC pattern making equipment both in wood and aluminum.

  • Expertise in design of suitable engineering and gating systems for simple to very complex and critical castings.
  • Dedicated team to support customers with optimum design for castings, tolerances, and mechanical properties etc.
  • Economically viable designing for manufacturing
  • High ratio of first time right development.
  • Dedicated CAD CAM 3D scanning & designing team.
  • Reduced lead time.
